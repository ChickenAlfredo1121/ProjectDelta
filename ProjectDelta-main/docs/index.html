How to Make Canvas' Calendar Better

Canvas is an app/website that is used by Universities as a class management and access system. Here students can see their classes, homework, policies, files, calendar, class related email, and so much more. Although, Canvas does fall short in some areas, particularly in its design. For instance, what I will be showing is the Canvas Calendar and ways to possibly improve upon its current version. There are numerous UX issues. Here you will see a possible idea for improved navigation, look, and function. Enjoy!
